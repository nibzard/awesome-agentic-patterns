## Patterns of Potential Hallucination

Based on this audit, common LLM hallucination patterns in academic references include:

1. **Venue Inflation**: Papers claimed at top venues (NeurIPS, ICLR) when only on arXiv
2. **Year Misattribution**: Papers with wrong publication years
3. **Title Approximation**: Close but not exact paper titles
4. **Author Misattribution**: Correct paper but wrong authors claimed
5. **Non-existent Papers**: Papers that don't exist on arXiv at all
